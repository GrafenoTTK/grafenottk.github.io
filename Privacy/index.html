Overview

At GrafenoTTK, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services, visit our website, or interact with our community.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our services, we may collect the following types of information:

• Community Interaction: Information you provide when participating in our Discord community, including your Discord username and messages.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. However, we may share non-personal, aggregated data for research, development, or analytical purposes.
