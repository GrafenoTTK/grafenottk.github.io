ABOUT US

Our Mission

GrafenoTTK is a collective dedicated to creating innovative solutions for performance optimization and advanced computing. Like our namesake graphene, we aim to develop lightweight yet powerful tools that enhance computing efficiency through open source collaboration.

Our Vision

We strive to democratize advanced optimization techniques and high-performance computing solutions. Our work spans operating systems, software optimization, and hardware acceleration, focusing on practical applications that empower users to maximize their existing resources.
